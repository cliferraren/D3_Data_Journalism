Correlation Between Poverty and Exercise

We want to investigate correlationship between a specific demographic like Poverty to behavioral risk factor like exercise activity. The surveyed data were collected from the American Community Survey (ACS) and the Behavioral Risk Factor Surveillance System (BRFSS) for every state in the United States for the year 2014.

The calculated correlation between Poverty and Exercise Activity is -0.67.
